Founder & President

Nahar Khan

Nahar Khan is the Founder and President of Solis Group, a strategic infrastructure and engagement platform operating across emerging and international markets. Under his leadership, Solis Group has grown to encompass four distinct verticals — engineering and consulting, media intelligence, strategic forums, and cultural programming.

With deep expertise in cross-border project delivery and stakeholder engagement, Nahar has built Solis into a trusted partner for governments, investors, and institutions seeking to create lasting impact in South Asia and beyond.
