Who We Are

A Strategic Infrastructure & Engagement Platform

Solis Group is a strategic infrastructure and engagement platform operating across emerging and international markets. We originate, structure, and support the delivery of high-impact infrastructure projects, working closely with government entities, foreign partners, and institutional stakeholders.

In parallel, we facilitate leadership forums and policy engagement that bring together policymakers, investors, and industry leaders, aligning capital, strategy, and implementation. By providing data-driven insights that inform decision-making, we strengthen partnerships and enable sustainable growth.

How We Work

We operate at the intersection of infrastructure development, strategic intelligence, and high-level convening — connecting the stakeholders, insights, and execution capabilities that turn ambition into delivered outcomes.

Whether supporting a single infrastructure project or coordinating multi-stakeholder initiatives across borders, we offer integrated solutions that combine local knowledge with global networks to deliver results.

Our Focus

  • Energy systems and climate infrastructure in Bangladesh
  • Regional policy intelligence for APAC investors and institutions
  • High-level diplomatic and cross-sector dialogue
  • Cultural exchange linking creativity with sustainable development
